As a coach, she partners with mid-career & senior professionals who are seeking accelerated personal & professional growth discover possibilities & pathways so that by acting on the same they can lead happier lives. Her core belief is that happier people create high performing organisations & a more compassionate world. She is skilled at both, individual and small group coaching.

Her coaching style is warm, encouraging, respectful, gently challenging and forward looking. She focuses on facilitating the identification of patterns and themes in behaviour and uncovering the underlying beliefs and mental models. She partners with her coachees and supports them to create clear, concrete, actionable development plans.

Her approach is to balance organizational priorities with individual aspirations and motivators, thus ensuring visible and lasting change as well as increased career success and personal satisfaction.

She specializes in performance, career and wellbeing coaching. In addition, she has expertise in and multiple success stories in working with women leaders, enabling them to connect with their values, leverage their strengths as well as find inner resources to fulfill their own aspirations and unlock their own possibilities.
